OSRLab is here to help you attain your professional and academic goals, wherever you may be with your skill levels. We believe the best way to learn is by doing, so let's get you started on a project today!

{% octicon beaker height:28 class:"fill-blue d-inline mr-2" aria-label:beaker %} Research

Every good research project begins with a literature search. Our online journal club wants to help you make sense of it all and give you a place to share the inspirations that come to you through your reading.

{% octicon comment-discussion height:28 class:"fill-blue d-inline mr-2" aria-label:comment-discussion %} Collaborate

Not sure how something works? Looking for a second opinion after a quick search reveals nothing? We're here to help. Our years of collective experience in academia and industry can help you accomplish your research goals and advance your career

A new kind of lab for a new age in science

There are a wealth of resources available to the public to help us engage in science across many disciplines and all skill levels. This truth is not reflected by the elitist attitudes of industry and academia, so we are creating a new space for public research in robotics to grow. We want to bridge the gap between citizen and scientist until the two are indistinguishable.
